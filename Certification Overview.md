## Course Summaries

### Course 1 — Product Management: An Introduction (14 hrs) ✅
Covers the foundational roles, responsibilities, and skills of a product manager. Examines the end-to-end product lifecycle and how product managers drive value creation. Explores career paths and certifications in the field.

**Labs completed:** Role of a Product Manager · Identifying How PM Can Help · Final Project — Product Development Scenario Review

---

### Course 2 — Product Management: Foundations & Stakeholder Collaboration (16 hrs) ✅
Focuses on communication and collaboration skills critical to product success. Covers RACI frameworks, SWOT analysis, stakeholder communication challenges, and team dynamics. Applied through real-world case studies.

**Labs completed:** Communication Issues & Solutions Worksheet · SWOT Analysis (Time is Now LLC Smartwatch) · RACI Matrix (Autonomous Truck AI System)

---

### Course 3 — Product Management: Initial Product Strategy and Plan (18 hrs) 🔄
Covers product concept development, market research, competitive analysis, and financial planning. Includes building a product roadmap and market requirements document (MRD).

**Deliverables:** Product concept · SWOT analysis · Market requirements document · Product roadmap · Business case financial analysis

---

### Course 4 — Product Management: Developing and Delivering a New Product (19 hrs) ⏳
Covers the full product development and launch process including pre-launch planning, beta testing, and go-to-market readiness. Focuses on building practical checklists for each phase of delivery.

**Deliverables:** Product development checklist · Beta testing plan · Launch readiness assessment · Post-launch review framework

---

### Course 5 — Introduction to Agile Development and Scrum (13 hrs) ⏳
Hands-on Agile methodology course using GitHub Projects and ZenHub. Covers sprint planning, user stories, product backlogs, burndown charts, and retrospectives. Builds a real Agile project in GitHub.

**Deliverables:** GitHub Kanban board · Product backlog · Sprint plan · Burndown chart · Sprint retrospective

---

### Course 6 — Product Management: Capstone Project (17 hrs) ⏳
Comprehensive final project applying the full product lifecycle — from concept through retirement. Builds a complete product management portfolio and prepares for the AIPMM CPM certification examination.

**Deliverables:** End-to-end product lifecycle portfolio · Selected PM artifacts · CPM exam readiness assessment

---

### Course 7 — Practice Exam for AIPMM Certified Product Manager (CPM) (4 hrs) ⏳
CPM certification preparation including exam eligibility requirements, content overview, and testing strategies. Includes a 40-question practice exam and a timed 120-question mock CPM exam.
