## Course Overview

This was the course where the connection between my professional background and product management became concrete. Course 3 focuses on the Conceive and Plan phases of the product lifecycle - the work that happens before anything gets built - covering how a product manager takes an idea and constructs the foundation that justifies investing in it.

The product used across all labs is the **Prep&Pivot App** - an AI agentic nutrition coach with integrated nutrition planning and tracking capabilities. The AI agent is the core of the product, proactively coaching users on nutrition goals, meal planning, and dietary adjustments rather than simply logging what they eat. Tracking and planning are the platform's capabilities; intelligent, personalized coaching is its purpose.

---

## Modules

**Module 1 — Product Conception and Planning Overview**
Introduces the Conceive and Plan phases and covers the tools used to evaluate and document a product concept, including SWOT analysis, product strategy options, and the Product Concept Document.

**Module 2 — Product Concept Identification**
Covers team building, the Tuckman Ladder model, the Initial Project Charter, market investigation, competitive analysis, personas, and the Market Requirements Document.

**Module 3 — Product Concept Investigation**
Focuses on market feedback, product vision and value proposition, preliminary launch strategy, risk identification, and the Business Case.

**Module 4 — Product Plan Phase**
Introduces the Plan Phase: developing a Product Roadmap, functional plans, marketing strategy updates, and a full Financial Analysis.

**Module 5 — Course Summary and Final Assessment**
Peer-reviewed final project applying all course concepts to a complete product management scenario.
